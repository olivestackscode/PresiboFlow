# PresiboFlow

**Open-source voice-first agentic AI for healthcare professionals in Africa.**

PresiboFlow helps doctors, clinics, and hospitals automate daily workflows using AI — turning spoken consultations and text inputs into actionable insights and tasks.

### 🌟 Key Features
- **AI-Powered Consultation Assistant** — Analyze clerking notes, suggest differentials, and generate insights.
- **Vital Signs Monitoring & Logging** — BP, heart rate, glucose, BMI, SpO2 with AI interpretation.
- **Automated Workflow Tools** — Create appointment letters, schedule follow-ups, and build care flows.
- **Drag & Drop Patient Flow Builder** — Medication reminders, checkup schedules, and alerts.
- **Voice-First Support** — Listen to consultations and extract clinical actions (vitals).
- **Privacy-First Design** — Runs locally with Ollama, no data leaves your computer.
- **Tailored for Nigerian Healthcare** — Supports resource-limited settings and common scenarios.

### 👥 Who It's For
- Doctors and medical officers
- Private clinics and hospitals
- Community health workers
- Non-medical healthcare administrators
